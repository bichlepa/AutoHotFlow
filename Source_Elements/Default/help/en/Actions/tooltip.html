Tooltip

The action Tooltip displays a tooltip with a message.

Settings

Text to show

Message shown to the user.

Duration

Specify how long the tooltip will be shown.

Options

If Follow mouse is selected, the tooltip will follow the mouse. If not, the tooltip will remain at its position.
If Follow mouse is selected, the current thread will wait until the tooltip disappears. If not, the current thread will continue right after the tooltip appears and will not wait until it disappears.
